About Healthcare Management

HIGH DEMAND. HIGH OPPORTUNITY.

Healthcare is a business and the industry needs professionals who are able to run operations. One of the country's fastest growing job markets, healthcare administrative occupations are expected to grow 18% by 2026, as employers look to fill tens of thousands of new positions according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. Healthcare managers in many areas of the field have a median annual income of $99,730.
