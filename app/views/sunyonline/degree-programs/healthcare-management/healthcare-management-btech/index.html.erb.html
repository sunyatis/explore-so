Healthcare Management BTech

The Bachelor of Technology in Healthcare Management (B. Tech) online is a technologically focused degree for those who are already working in the health care field. Individuals in the health care management degree online may currently be working in hospitals in technical roles such as laboratory, radiology, records, occupational therapy, surgical technology, paramedic, ultrasound, or a variety of administrative roles, and seeking advancement into management or administrative positions. A student who has earned an associate degree or 60 credits in a health, such as the AS in Healthcare Studies, or business related area will be prepared to complete a bachelor's degree.

The HCM-BT is an online degree for working adults that will provide them with flexible online courses in healthcare, a pathway to completion, and course work focused in healthcare and management. The healthcare degree degree offers students multiple start dates throughout the year to fit their need.

The online healthcare management program emphasizes the development of managerial skills through a set of core courses and a wide array of electives to address areas such as healthcare finance, the culture of healthcare, human resources, communications, healthcare law, ethics, policies, information systems, marketing, and quality control.

This Bachelor of Technology in Healthcare Management online degree also provides an opportunity for students to continue their education toward an MBA or MHA and to become a CNO, CEO, or COO.

About the Program

Healthcare management is one of the country’s fastest growing job markets with jobs expected to grow 18% by 2026 as employers look to fill tens of thousands of new positions. This program will open doors to management careers in the healthcare industry through flexible online courses focused in healthcare and management.

  • Multiple start dates with online health care courses offered in 4- to 7-week sessions to help you better focus on each course
  • Summer and winter courses offered online so you can finish faster
  • Courses developed and taught by high quality faculty with industry experience
  • Online courses in 
    • Business Ethics
    • Strategic & Creative Prob Solving
    • Project Mgmt for Business Professionals
    • Principles of Leadership
    • Strategic Marketing
    • Legal Environment of Business
  • Assignments challenge you to connect the course content with the field
  • Opportunities are built in to make connections with industry professionals
  • Faculty and Advisors available to meet virtually or via phone to help you be successful
  • Graduates go on to work in the health care field as
    • Medical and health services department managers
    • Public relations and fundraising managers
    • Administrative services managers
    • Training and development managers
  • Gain business and healthcare skills that will help you be well-rounded manager
  • ASC Ranked #1 in New York for online Healthcare Management - Healthcare Administration EDU
  • Create the foundation for a pathway to an Healthcare Adminstration MBA online with SUNY Online, Healthcare Leadership MBA Online, or an MHA
