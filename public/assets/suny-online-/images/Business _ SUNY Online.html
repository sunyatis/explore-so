Fast Facts About Business

A career in business is versatile.

The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) reports that wages for many business-related occupations are higher than those for all workers, and growth in the business sector is expected to grow 5% by 2029.

The top skills employers look for are ones developed in an online business program.

Graduates have two MBA options - MBA with a GMAT requirement and MBA without a GMAT requirement.
